Common Questions from Businesses

Do we provide installation services?

No, Schön Mobility sells the chargers but does not handle installations. Businesses should contact a local electrician for installation services.

What is needed for installation?

Businesses typically need conduits to accommodate the power supply to the chargers. Each circuit should support 32 amps and 240 volts for the charger to function correctly. We recommend providing the electrician with the specification sheet to ensure all necessary work is completed properly.
